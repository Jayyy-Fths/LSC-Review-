Liberty Science Center Trip

Justin Manley

3/25/26

Introduction

When we arrived to LSC, we went to go to the robotics area, and then we coded a little robot. After that we went to lunch, and got an overpriced meal. Then we went to the planetarium, and seem some cool planets and galaxies. We had some freetime after the planetarium, so we ended up walking around, seeing fishes, animals, and other things. Then that was the end of the trip, and we ended up being late getting back to the school.



When I was first going there, I was expecting to have a lot of fun, and being able to code. I was also hoping to be able to see the T.Rex exhibit as well as the planetarium.

Top 3 Highlights

Highlight 1

This was all of us inside the planetarium, and my favorite part of this trip was just being able to hang out as a class, and not having to do any actual work.

Highlight 2

Going to the planetarium was really cool, being able to see different planets and the galaxies. I was interested in space since I was a kid so this was really nice to see.

Highlight 3

Seeing the skyline of the city through Liberty Science Center was awesome. I always liked the idea of the city, where anything can happen.

Final Rating

⭐ 8/10

I thought that the experience was actually really nice, and that there were a lot of things to do there. The only complaint that I had was that the food and the things in the gift shop were extremely overpriced. But overall, it was a fun experience to do things with friends, especially the planetarium since we were all having fun in there too.

Reflection

What did I learn?

I learned how to code a sphero robot, and also learned more about the universe we live in.

What surprised me?

How much everything costed, since I was expecting it to be expensive, but not ten dollars for a magnet.

Would I recommend this trip?

I would definetly recommend this trip to someone else, since there were alot of things to see and do.
