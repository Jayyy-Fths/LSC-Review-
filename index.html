Introduction

At Liberty Science Center, we started in robotics and coded a small robot. After lunch, we went to the planetarium and saw planets and galaxies. Later, we explored exhibits with animals and other science displays before heading back to school.

Before the trip, I expected to have fun, do some coding, and visit the planetarium. The planetarium ended up being one of the best parts.
