Liberty Science Center Trip

Justin Manley · March 25, 2026

Introduction

When we arrived to LSC, we started in the robotics area and coded a small robot. After that we had lunch, then we went to the planetarium and saw planets and galaxies. We had some free time after that, so we explored and saw fish, animals, and other exhibits before heading back to school.

Before the trip, I expected to have fun and do some coding. I was also hoping to see the T-Rex exhibit and visit the planetarium.

Top 3 Highlights

Planetarium with Friends

My favorite part was getting to hang out with everyone in the planetarium.

Space Visuals

Seeing the planets and galaxies was really cool and matched my interest in space.

Skyline View

The view of the city skyline from the center was one of my favorite photo moments.
