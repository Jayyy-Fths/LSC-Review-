Field Trip Review

A Day at Liberty Science Center

By JA · March 25, 2026

This page captures what we did, what stood out, and how the trip felt overall—from robotics and space visuals to the final reflection.

Trip Timeline

  1. Robotics Lab

    We started in robotics and got to code a small robot, which was the most hands-on part of the day.

  2. Lunch Break

    Food options were available, but prices felt high for what was offered.

  3. Planetarium Session

    The planetarium visuals of planets and galaxies were one of the coolest experiences on the trip.

  4. Free Explore Time

    We walked through exhibits and saw different animals and displays before heading back.

Top Highlights

Three moments that made the trip memorable.

Planetarium Atmosphere

Hanging out together in the planetarium made the trip feel different from a normal school day.

Space Visuals

The projected journey through planets and galaxies was impressive and matched my interest in space.

City View

Seeing the skyline from the center was a great visual break and one of my favorite photo moments.

Final Score

8/10

The trip had a lot to do and a strong mix of technology, science visuals, and social time. The only downside was the cost of food and gift-shop items.

Reflection

  • What did I learn?

    I learned how to code a sphero robot and understood more about space and the universe.

  • What surprised me?

    How expensive things were compared with what I expected before the trip.

  • Would I recommend this trip?

    Yes. There were many exhibits to explore, and it was a good experience with classmates.
